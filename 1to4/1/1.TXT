        Lesson 1   Excuse me!
                 对不起！

Listen to the tape then answer this question. Whose handbag is it?
听录音，然后回答问题，这是谁的手袋？

  Excuse me!
  Yes?
  Is this your handbag?
  Pardon?
  Is this your handbag? 
  Yes, it is.
  Thank you very much.
